About Me

My name is Joy Wamaitha, and I’m from Kenya. My passion for tech ignited when I joined the CodeHive program in 2022. Immersed in the world of programming and design, I discovered a thrilling journey that’s been both challenging and rewarding. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills through hands-on projects and internships. I have experience building and integrating APIs, managing databases, and developing secure backend systems. I’m skilled in front-end development with React, and I excel in data analysis using Python and Pandas. Additionally, I have expertise in using AWS technologies like Lambda, EC2, DynamoDB, and S3 to optimize cloud systems. If you’re looking for someone who brings creativity and tech magic to the table, I’m your go-to. Let’s create, innovate, and have fun while making awesome things happen!
